Projects

elmylonas.gr — Λευτέρης Μυλωνάς

elmylonas.gr is the official digital hub of Λευτέρης Μυλωνάς, an economist and secondary-education teacher. The website was built as a complete professional presence with both public-facing content for students and institutions, and a private administration environment for content management.

The platform combines a modern, responsive frontend with a secure backend and admin panel. The administrator can log in, upload and update teaching material, manage exam exercises, and publish announcements without touching code. This transforms the site from a static portfolio into a living resource that evolves with the educator’s work.

On the frontend, visitors can easily explore academic background, downloadable teaching resources, and tutoring information, with a design that emphasizes clarity, trust, and accessibility. Integrated contact options (email, phone, social links) and remote learning support (Zoom) ensure seamless communication with students.
